Release Notes: Napkin Look & Feel

Things You Ought To Know

Here are some things you ought to know if you are going to use the Napkin Look & Feel in your work.
  • Napkin does not work with JDK 1.3. The PLAF framework is quite different between the two.
  • It takes time to draw all the variations that make up the "hand drawn" look of Napkin. You will therefore notice that it is rather slower than other L&Fs, although it seems quite usable on a "modern" system. I'm sure this could be made better, but I think it will be inevitable.
  • JViewport requires "simple" scrolling (JViewport.SIMPLE_SCROLL_MODE). So it will actively ignore any other mode you set, although it will remember the other mode and restore it when you swtich away from Napkin.
  • You will not get handwritten text (by default) in HTML, or at least if you do that is platform dependent. This is not possible to set in JDK 1.4, but will be in JDK 1.5.
